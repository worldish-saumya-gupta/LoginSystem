Password validation

A password should be alphanumeric.
First letter of the password should be capital.
Password must contain a special character (@, $, !, &, etc).
Password length between 6 to 20 characters.
One of the most important that the password fields should not be empty.

Email validation

The presence of an “@” symbol, separating the local part from the domain part.
The presence of a domain part with at least one dot, indicating a domain extension.
No leading, trailing, or consecutive whitespace characters within the email address.

If it does match, send an alert stating “valid email address”
If it doesn’t match, send an alert stating “invalid email address”
